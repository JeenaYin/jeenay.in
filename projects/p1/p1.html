Automated Lighting System

I built a lighting system using max msp to analyze audio input through microphone in realtime, and visualize the audio information through a group of 30 LED lights.

The amplitude of the input directly triggers the saturation of the colors. The ratio of high and low frequencies in the audio controls the RGB value, such that the more lower frequency there is, the more pinkish the color is.

The delay effect is made by grouping the 30 lights into 3 subgroups, so that the changes of color happen first at the inner lights, and then propogate outwards.

This lighting system is made for a piece I composed for the CMU Exploded Ensemble.
